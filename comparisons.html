HVI evolution

In this page we plot the HVI evolution of several implementations running on various benchmark functions. We omitted ELL1, ELLI2, CIGTAB1, CIGTAB2 since in Anguilla (v0.0.18) and Shark (modified 4.1) we changed the parameters that affect the conditioning.
